About me

Hey there! I'm Sandeep Solanki, an Associate Software Engineer at Bitsquad Software who loves turning ideas into beautiful, responsive, and interactive web experiences. With expertise in Angular, React.js, Node.js, .NET Framework, HTML, CSS, and TypeScript, I thrive on building sleek, user-friendly, and scalable applications that make an impact. I hold a Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) from NIT Raipur and have grown through hands-on roles as a Frontend Developer at Uraan SoftSkills and Mentoraide, working on projects from a Scientific Calculator built with pure JavaScript to a Movie Recommendation System powered by Python. I'm also expanding into cloud technologies with Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and sharpening my project management and planning skills alongside my technical work. I’m always eager to learn and push the boundaries of full-stack development. Whether it's solving DSA problems, architecting a full-stack solution, or optimizing performance, I strive to create seamless and engaging digital experiences. When I'm not coding, you’ll find me geeking out over new tech, refining my design skills, or brainstorming my next exciting project. Let’s connect and build something awesome together! 🚀
